Abstract

This ontology models medical devices, risks, incidents, manufacturers and regulatory risk classes in the healthcare domain.

Introduction back to ToC

The Medical Device Catalog Ontology has been developed to represent medical devices, associated risks, incidents, corrective actions, manufacturers and regulatory classifications. Its goal is to support the organization, documentation and reuse of knowledge related to medical device safety.
